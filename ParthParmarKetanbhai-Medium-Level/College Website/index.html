Main Entrance

L. D. College of Engineering endearingly known as L.D.C.E is Ahmedabad’s premier engineering college situated at the city’s heart and surrounded by elite organization like PRL, ATIRA, ISRO, IIM and CEPT. Starting in 1948 to impart quality higher education in various fields of engineering, it has seen unprecedented growth.

It is affiliated with Gujarat Technological University & administrated by Department of Technical Education, Government of Gujarat.

Vision

To contribute for sustainable development of nation through achieving excellence in technical education and research while facilitating transformation of students into responsible citizens and competent professionals.

Mission

To impart affordable and quality education in order to meet the needs of industries and achieve excellence in teaching-learning process.

To create a conducive research ambience that drives innovation and nurtures research-oriented scholars and outstanding professionals.

Heritage

Our university has a rich history dating back to its founding in 1856. Over the years, we have established ourselves as a leading institution in higher education, renowned for our commitment to academic excellence and innovation.

In the early 20th century, the university expanded its campus to accommodate a growing student body, adding iconic buildings that blend historic architecture with modern facilities. These structures not only serve as a testament to our past but also provide state-of-the-art amenities for today's students.

We are proud of our alumni who have gone on to achieve great things in various fields, contributing to the betterment of society. Their success stories inspire current students to pursue their dreams with dedication and integrity.

Throughout its history, the university has maintained a tradition of fostering a diverse and inclusive community. We believe that a wide range of perspectives enriches the educational experience and prepares our graduates to thrive in a globalized world.

Join us as we continue to build on this legacy, shaping the future through education, research, and community engagement.

Festivities

Founders' Day

Date: January 15
Description: Founders' Day is a celebration of our university's founding and its achievements over the years. Join us for a day of commemorative events, speeches, and a grand banquet.

Spring Festival

Date: March 20
Description: The Spring Festival marks the arrival of spring with various cultural performances, food stalls, and fun activities. It's a time to enjoy the vibrant campus life and embrace new beginnings.

Graduation Ceremony

Date: June 10
Description: The Graduation Ceremony honors the hard work and achievements of our graduating students. Families and friends are invited to celebrate this milestone with the graduates.

Homecoming Week

Date: September 15-21
Description: Homecoming Week is a week-long celebration that welcomes alumni back to campus. Events include a parade, football game, alumni reunions, and various social gatherings.

Winter Gala

Date: December 5
Description: The Winter Gala is a formal event that marks the end of the year. Enjoy an elegant evening of dinner, dancing, and entertainment as we celebrate the holiday season and the year's accomplishments.
